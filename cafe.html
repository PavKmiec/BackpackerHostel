Backpacker's Cafe

Our Backpacker's Cafe is open to residents as well as visitors. We have wide range of meals that are always freshley prepared. From hearty breakfasts to delicious dinners and tasty food on the go, we recognise good food when we see it…

From 1 to 100 we offer a great range of food and drink for groups and hungry hostellers, but remember to book in advance! We offer a delicious highland breakfast, packed lunches and evening meals. Our hostel is licensed, selling a range of teas, coffees and soft drinks along with a selection of wines, cider, lager and local Scottish beers. Self-catering facilities are aslo available.
