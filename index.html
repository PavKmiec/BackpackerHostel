Best Location

The Hostel has one of the best locations in Scotland. From the attractive loch-side garden, the views across to mountain range are breathtaking. Popular with walkers and tourist alike...

Most celebrated scenery!

There are otters, badgers and pine martins in the area. Red deer are numerous, and on the mountains ravens, ptarmigan, golden eagles and mountain hares can be seen on occasion...

Relaxation

With no mobile phone reception the emphasis here is on tranquillity and relaxation! ... and also well situated for walking and exploring some of Scotland's most celebrated scenery!
